Bottle Guard

Soil Type

Grown in wide range of soil types , but it thrives well in sandy loam soil.

Nutrient Recommendation

Adding Organic matter or Farm Yield manure makes soil rich.

sowing time

June to July for monsoon or rainy crops in plains and April in the hills.

January to end of february for summer crops.

seed rate

3.5 - 6kg/ha

seed treatment

Bottle guard seeds should be treated with Pseudomonas flourences 10 grams.

Method of sowing

Dibbing method.

spacing

2m to 3 * 1.0 * 1.5m.

Water requirements

Drip irrigation is most important in bottle guard cultivation.

Harvesting time

Should be harvested when they are still green.

yield

100 - 120 quintal/hectare
