Tomato


Season

Kharif and Rabi crop

Soil type

It can be grown in varied type of soil ranging from sandy loam to clay, black soil and red soil having proper drainage.

climate

warm and dry weather

temperature

10-25°C

Required moisture content

The soil needs to be damp at least 6 - 8 inches below the ground to stimulate root growth.# required region - tropical and subtropical region 

Sowing Nethod

Transplanting of seedling in main field

Seed Treatement

To protect crop from soil borne disease and pest, before sowing do seed treatment with Thiram@3gm or Carbendazim @ 3gm of seeds. After chemical treatment, treat seed with Trichoderma@5gm/kg of seeds.  keep it in shade. And use it for sowing.

Seed Rate

Use seed rate of 100gm for preparing seedling for sowing in one acre land.

Nutrient recommendation

Nitrogen:60kg/acre, Phosphorus:25kg/acre, Potash: 25kg/acre
