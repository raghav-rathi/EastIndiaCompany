Introduction

Bhendi is one of the popular vegetable in India. It is cultivated extensively all the year round for its immature fruits. Bhendi fruits are used as a vegetable. The stem of the plant is used for the extraction of the fiber.

Climate and Cultivation

Bhendi is a warm season vegetable crop and requires a long warm growing season. In India it is grown is summer months and during the rainy season. It is highly sensitive to frost. Seeds germinate poorly at ground temperature of 20 Â°C or less.
